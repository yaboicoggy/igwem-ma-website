Brand Guidelines: IGWEM.MA
Version: 2.0

Identity Focus: Professionalism, Modern Administration, Trust.

1. Brand Identity & Logo
The IGWEM.MA visual identity is built on a "Modern Administration" concept, utilizing sharp geometric lines and a sophisticated, warm metallic palette.

Logo Variations
Primary Logo: The full wordmark "IGWEM.MA" with the icon to the left. The "MA" and icon use the Copper accent.

Secondary Logo: "MODERN ADMINISTRATION" subtext placed directly beneath the wordmark in Burgundy.

Isolated Icon: The stylized 'A' / geometric peak icon used for social media avatars and favicons.

Clearspace
Definition: Maintain a minimum clearspace around the logo equal to the height of the "X" in the IGWEM wordmark.

Guideline: Ensure no text or graphic elements encroach on this margin to maintain visual integrity.

2. Color Palette
The transition from V1 (Neon) to V2 (Classic) moves the brand into a premium, authoritative space.

Element	Color Name	HEX Code	Usage
Primary	Burgundy	#8B0000	Main logo text, primary headers, button backgrounds.
Accent	Copper	#D9A441	Icon highlights, "MA" text, active button states.
Background	Cream/White	#FDFCF8	Primary background for documents and web.
Neutral	Dark Grey	#333333	Body text and secondary typography.
Neutral	Light Grey	#E5E5E5	Dividers, borders, and UI backgrounds.
3. Typography
A clean, sans-serif pairing that balances modern tech with corporate readability.

Headings: Montserrat
Style: Bold or Semi-Bold.

Usage: Logo wordmark, section headers, and callouts.

Tracking: Standard (0).

Body Text: Open Sans
Style: Regular or Light.

Usage: Paragraphs, documentation, and long-form data.

Readability: High legibility for administrative reports and web content.

4. Iconography & UI Elements
Icons and buttons should follow a minimalist, "clean-cut" aesthetic.

Icon Style: * Line-based or solid geometric shapes.

Color: Primary Burgundy or Copper accents.

Background: Placed on light grey or cream circular/square containers.

Button Style:

Primary: Solid Burgundy background with White or Cream text. Rounded corners (approx. 8px radius).

Secondary/Action: Copper border or background to denote specific user actions.

Label: Uppercase "ACTION BUTTON" or "DONE RIGHT" in Montserrat.

5. Brand Slogan
Modern Administration

Font: Montserrat Medium Italic.

Primary Color: Burgundy.
